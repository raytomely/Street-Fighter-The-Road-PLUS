f = forward
b = backward
u = up
d = down
p = punch

Ken:
fireball --> d f p
dragon punch  --> f d f p
hurricane kicks --> b f p
axe kick --> u u p or d d p

Guile:
fireball --> b f p
somersault Kick --> d u p

Iori:
ground fireball --> d f p
crescent of tainted flame --> f d f p

all characters can perform dash attack: --> f f p
